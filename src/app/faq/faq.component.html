About us

TDX is a launchpad with highly vetted projects where users can invest $100-$500 without the need to stake like in other launchpads. This level of access along with the focus on highly vetting projects that launch positions TDX as one of the year's most highly anticipated platforms for retail investors looking for the same access as VCs.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I participate in Deals?
  1. Sign up with your email address.
  2. Complete the KYC
  3. Go to the project page in which you want to invest
  4. Click on apply now button
  5. Complete the payment
Through which cryptocurrencies can I invest on TDX?

You can invest using USDT, USDC and BUSD.
What is the minimum investment amount required to invest in Deals?

The minimum investment is $100 to participate in TDX projects.

What if my wallet transfer doesn’t clear before the token sale closes?

If the transfer doesn’t arrive before the sale deadline, we cannot guarantee your allocation. As our token allocation will be provided on a first-come-first-serve basis.

How is the conversion price determined?
We display an estimated price on the Deals sale board. If you deposit crypto into your TDX wallet immediately after completing this step, it should be close to the actual conversion rate.
When will I find out whether I got the tokens?

The allocations will only be calculated and released shortly after the registration period ends.

How can I perform the KYC on TDX?

The user has to click on the KYC button, which shows in the user profile.

Then the user is processed through the KYC journey, which includes face matching and ID doc verification.

Afterwards, a team of specialized operators will verify the documents in any given language. If there are no additional documents required, the user receives an approval email and the status of a verified user.

How soon will the fund reflect on my TDX wallet?

The funds will reflect as soon as you deposit them on the TDX wallet.
It may at times take longer due to network congestion among other reasons.
Please ensure you have entered the correct wallet address.
